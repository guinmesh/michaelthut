ABOUT

My name is Michael Thut and I am in my second year of the 3-year M.Arch program at the University of Michigan. Prior to my time at Michigan, I studied both Math and Studio Art at Swarthmore College. These have become foundational to my architecture education and my creative practice. While this book is intended to be presentation of my creative work to this date, it is also a manifestation of me and my interests. I believe the logic, systemization, craft, and attention to detail that are a part of my personality are on display in this body of work. I hope you enjoy my practice!
